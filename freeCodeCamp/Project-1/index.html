Prof. Rachid Yazami

FOUNDING EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR & C.T.O.

Here's a time line of Prof. Rachid Yazami

  • 1953 - Born in Fes, Morocco
  • 1971 - Baccalaureate in mathematical sciences.
  • 1972 - Arrived in France
  • 1978 - Engineer in Electrochemistry, Institute National Polytech
  • 1985 - Doctor of Philosophy in Electrochemistry
  • 1985 - Associate research director National Center for Scientific Research, Grenoble
  • 1992 - Consultant, Grenoble
  • 1998 - research director
  • 1988—1990 - Invited scientist Kyoto University and Shinshu University, Japan
  • 2000 - Visiting associate California Institute of Technology Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Pasadena, California
  • 2004—2006 - Director National Center for Scientific Research-Caltech International Laboratory
  • 2007 - Founder Contour Energy Inc., Azuza, California
  • 2010 - Principal scientist Nanyang Technological University, Singapore
  • 2014 - Holder of several awards and distinctions, he is decorated by H.M. King Mohammed VI of Wissam Al Kafaa Al Fikria
  • 2014 - winner of the Charles Stark Draper Award from the National Academy of Engineering (NAE)
  • 2016 - received an award as a finalist of the Marius Lavet Prize of Inventing-Engineers in Paris, France.
  • July 14, 2016 - Yazami was awarded the title of Chevalier de la Legion of honor of France.
  • 2017 - received the Honors Award of the Moroccans of the World
  • 2018 - the "Scientific and Technological Innovation" Prize, as part of the "Takrim 2018" initiative, which was awarded to him in Kuwait.
  • 2019 - received the Arab Investor Award in the “Green Application” category.
  • 2020 - won the Mohammed bin Rashid Medal for Scientific Excellence in the UAE
